PLOT

An unnamed 34-year-old Japanese NEET is evicted from his home following his parents' death and skipping the funeral. Upon some self-introspection, he concluded his life was ultimately pointless but still intercepts a speeding truck heading towards a group of teenagers in an attempt to do something meaningful for once in his life and manages to pull one of them out of harm's way before dying. Awakening in a baby's body, he realizes his being reincarnated in a world of sword and sorcery and resolves to become successful in his new life, discarding his past identity for his new life as Rudeus Greyrat. Due to inherited affinity and early training, Rudeus becomes highly skilled at magic. During his childhood, he becomes a student of demon magician Roxy Migurdia, a friend to demihuman Sylphiette, and a magic teacher to noble heiress Eris Boreas Greyrat.

Rudeus Greyrat

VA: Yumi Uchiyama / Madeleine Morris

He was an overweight 34-year-old Japanese NEET who is reincarnated into another world after being hit by a truck. Realizing he wasted away his previous life, he resolves to live this new one to its fullest.


He is extremely self-aware and humble because he does not want to revert to his old self. He is extremely perverted and even though he has this shortcomings, his family and friends still love him since he always takes care of them to a great extent.

Roxy Migurdia

VA: Kohara Konomi / Rojas Michelle

Roxy is a talented Migurd mage, and a former magic tutor. Because she can't use telepathy, she leaves her village due to feeling isolated from her peers. She becomes a travelling tutor and eventually becomes Rudeus' teacher.


Roxy is a calm and collected person with a wise outlook on life, yet always seeking to improve herself. She is very intelligent, able to piece together information and make rational deductions.

Sylphiette

VA: Ai Kayano / Emily Neves

Sylphiette is Rudeus' childhood friend who is part human, elf, and beast race. She had short green hair and elfish ears. Her tomboyish appearance initially made Rudeus believe she was a boy.


Sylphiette was very shy and meek. She was very dependent on Rudeus and would break down crying if Rudeus were to leave her. After Rudeus was forcefully sent away to act as Eris' home tutor, she resolved herself to learn and train in order to support Rudeus in the future.

Eris Boreas Greyrat

VA: Ai Kakuma / Lindsay Seidel

Eris Boreas Greyrat is a noble girl and second cousin of Rudeus. She is a tsundere girl with a short temper but has potential in the Sword God Style.


Upon meeting Rudeus, Eris was contemptuous, impulsive, defiant, and condescending; she is also short tempered and prone to violent outbursts. Eris also has a high level of shyness, often hitting Rudeus for touching her suddenly. During her journey to return home following the Teleport Incident, she grows to love Rudeus.

Paul Greyrat

VA: Toshiyuki Morikawa / Jared Smith

Paul Greyrat is the father of Rudeus Greyrat, Norn Greyrat, and Aisha Greyrat. He is both a talented man that became advanced in all three sword styles at a young age. He formed a party called Fangs of the Black Wolf consisting of six members, him included. Sometime later, he got married to one of his team members, Zenith, who gave birth to Rudeus and they settle in Buena Village.


Paul is a confident, bold, and playful man. Paul is also a lustful person drawn to women with large breasts. Paul wasn't a bad dad, but he wasn't a good person.

Zenith Greyrat

VA: Hisako Kanemoto / Bryn Apprill

Zenith Greyrat is Paul's wife and mother to Rudeus and Norn. Born a noble, she ran away from home and joined Paul's party before starting a family with him later. While trying to find a job as a Healer, she was later saved by Paul. She then joined Paul's party, the Fangs of the Black Wolf, as a healer.


Zenith has a very warm and loving personality, acting as a loving mother who can be strict at times. She is very conservative in morals as a follower of Millis, and will get angry at Paul due to the fact that he is a womanizer.

Ruijerd Superdia

VA: Daisuke Namikawa / Christopher Wehkamp

Ruijerd Superdia is the former leader of the Superd's Warrior group who once served under Laplace with unwavering loyalty. He was accompanying Rudeus and Eris but left in search of his people. Ruijerd travels the world to dissolve the stigma against the Superd race.


Ruijerd is very kind and fatherly. He always helps children no matter what the circumstances, and would go berserk when people try to hurt them. Ruijierd follows a very strict warrior's code of honor that he would go to extreme lengths to fulfill his promises.

Ghislaine Dedoldia

VA: Megumi Toyoguchi / Rachel Robinson

Ghislaine Dedoldia is a talented swordsman from the beast race, the mentor of Eris, the student of Rudeus Greyrat in magic and other subjects. She is one of the few people who have become a Sword King. After becoming a Sword Saint, she traveled with Paul Greyrat's Fangs of the Black Wolf party as a warrior.


Ghislaine was described as a strong-willed individual and a meathead by Paul. She is looking after Eris and listening to her problems. She is deeply loyal to Sauros and Eris, who saved her when she was on the brink of death.
